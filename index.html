About me

Mofassal Al Alif In over ten years, Mofassal Al Alif has established himself as one of the famous dance artists in Bangladesh. Embracing an artistic vision that both respects and challenges Indian Classical form and Contemporary Dance. After having awarded as a dance artist, he joined The Terence Lewis Contemporary Dance Company that is one of the most famous dance companies in the world for higher education in dance. He is working as a performer and choreographer in TV, stage and film media simultaneously
